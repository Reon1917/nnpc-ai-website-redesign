title: "Our Origin"
---
NNPC Consulting Co., Ltd., a subsidiary of Chin Chun Enterprise, a traditional manufacturing powerhouse specializing in material and adhesive lamination, has been on a journey of digital transformation for over a decade. Under the leadership of Derek, who brings a strong industrial engineering background, the company recognized the critical role of efficiency and data in driving profitability. This realization fueled their commitment to implementing smart manufacturing practices within their own operations.

Drawing upon the rich manufacturing experience of our parent company and our own successful implementation journey, we deeply understand the pressing need for smart manufacturing and the challenges businesses face during transformation. We have assembled a team of experts from diverse fields to share our valuable insights and cutting-edge solutions with the industry.

Based in Thailand, we are dedicated to introducing advanced solutions to help local businesses achieve smart upgrades and compete on the global stage. By empowering companies to enhance their competitiveness, we aim to stimulate the entire supply chain, injecting new momentum into Thailand's industries and creating value and employment opportunities.
